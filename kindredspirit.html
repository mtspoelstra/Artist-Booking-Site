KindredSpirit is a dynamic musical act of multi-talented musicians, poets, and creative artists from Syracuse, NY. The core group consists of Nick Abelgore, Daniel Fields, Nick Fields, Marcus Oliver, and Daniel Pugh.

Combining elements of pop, funk, classic rock, jazz, and classical music, Kindred Spirit embodies an ever-transforming sound founded on spontaneity and intuition. Every performance is unique, rendering the familiar as new and the new as familiar. The group exists as several different configurations of personnel (duo, trio, full band), and can be found playing around CNY in churches, nursing homes, restaurants, clubs, bars, and wedding bands.

KindredSpirit has been featured in Civic Morning Musicals “First Mondays Series” and has residency at “The Fitz” speakeasy in Downtown Syracuse. KindredSpirit also brings Kirtan (Call and Response chanting inspired by the Yoga tradition) in collaboration with Vyana Yoga.

We are ecstatic to bring a program of original music, and jazz-pop-rock fusion to the Oneida Arts and Heritage Center.
